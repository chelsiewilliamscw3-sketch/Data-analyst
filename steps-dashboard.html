Project Summary

Built a product analytics simulation that tracks performance and adoption across operational workflows. Prepared data with SQL and Excel, then visualized KPIs in Power BI—processing time, transaction volume, error rates, feature utilization, and allocated cost per transaction. Included target-vs-actual comparisons and region/department drill-downs.

Data & SQL

features.csv users.csv transactions.csv costs_monthly.csv targets.csv SQL: schema & KPI queries

Key Metrics

  • Transactions over time & feature utilization share
  • Average processing time (cycle hours) vs. targets
  • Error/failed transaction rate by month & feature
  • Allocated cost per transaction (monthly costs ÷ volume)
  • Region × Department performance snapshot

Insights & Recommendations

  • Cycle time decreased MoM after release v2.x; maintain rollout cadence.
  • Error clusters concentrated in Document Upload during peak hours; prioritize validation fixes.
  • South region shows longer cycle times; review staffing and training plans.
  • Cost per transaction trending down with volume; continue optimizing infra spend.
